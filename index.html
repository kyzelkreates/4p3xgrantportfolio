The problem: wrong-vehicle routing

Bridge strikes are a serious, preventable safety and infrastructure problem in Britain. Wrong vehicle routing causes bridge strikes, delays, damage, disruption, insurance costs, and increased emissions from detours. Network Rail has reported that bridge strikes remain a frequent and costly problem, causing disruption and financial cost to the rail industry.

Source: Network Rail "Wise Up, Size Up" campaign and bridge-strike safety reporting for 2024/25.

What Big V's Best Routes™ demonstrates

  • Vehicle profile checks (height, width, weight)
  • Route risk review and awareness flagging
  • Driver safety check-in and advisory warnings
  • Evidence capture and incident reporting
  • Supervisor dashboard and oversight tools

⚠️ Important safety disclaimer

Big V's Best Routes™ is an advisory safety-support system. It does not replace legal route planning, professional judgement, official highway data, driver responsibility, or transport compliance duties. All routing and safety decisions remain the legal responsibility of the driver, operator, and relevant transport and highway authorities. No AI system can guarantee legal compliance or the prevention of bridge strikes. Human oversight, professional verification, and official data sources must always be used for live routing decisions.

Other safety-relevant products

ResponseLink OS™ for welfare visit safety. Recharge Burnout Recovery™ for mental health early support. Quantum Compliance OS™ for compliance and audit evidence. TrustSheild OS™ for crisis response coordination.

Responsible AI commitments

All 4P3X Intelligent AI™ advisory systems operate within bounded, defined roles. No system makes autonomous decisions. Human review is mandatory for any safety-critical outcome. AI boundaries are documented and safeguarding controls are built in before live deployment.

Measurable safety pilot metrics
